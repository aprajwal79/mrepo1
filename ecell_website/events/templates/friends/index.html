Young Entrepreneur's Ability Haul

It's a Startup expo (start up showcase) in which participants have to inculcate self-efficacy, locus of control and level of resilience to ensure the plan which they initialized as a startup, aimed at providing college students with an opportunity to learn about the process of starting a business/Startup. The event will feature startups from various places and domain. The main motto of the event is to educate students about entrepreneurship by inculcating the essence of innovation and acknowledging their attained experiences.

About Us

The cell always lends a helping hand to the budding entrepreneurs and innovative minds. We start from identifying ideas and go towards nurturing them.

Our Vision



We aim at building innovative, enterprising and thereby self-reliant India. We strive to create an impact to the society by instigating a change.

Our Mission



E&I-Cell, NIT Andhra Pradesh is dedicated to fostering innovative ideas and entrepreneurial spirit within the institute. We provide a platform for creative and igniting minds to project their scientific, technological, managerial skills and help them mould their ideas into reality.
